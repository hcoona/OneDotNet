amazing

adjective
 
/əˈmeɪzɪŋ/
/əˈmeɪzɪŋ/
jump to other results
  1.  
     very surprising, especially in a way that you like or admire 令人大为惊奇的；（尤指）令人惊喜（或惊羡、惊叹）的 synonym astonishing, astounding, incredible
    • an amazing feat/story/experience 惊人的壮举/故事/经历
    • That's amazing, isn't it? 真是令人惊叹，是不是？
    • The amazing thing is, he really believes he'll get away with it. 令人惊异的是他真的相信自己能够逃脱惩罚。
    • amazing to do something It was truly amazing to see how much work goes into a Broadway musical. 看到一部百老汇音乐剧有这么多的作品，真是令人惊讶。
    • it is amazing how, what, etc… It's amazing how quickly people adapt. 人适应环境的速度之快真是惊人。
    • it is amazing that… I find it amazing that people can be so irresponsible. 人们可以如此不负责任，我对此感到惊讶。
    • It’s amazing the difference a few polite words make. 客气一下竟有如此不同，真让人想不到。
    Extra Examples

    • The market has an amazing variety of fish. 市场上有各种各样的鱼。
    • The service is amazing value for money. 这项服务物超所值。
    • The virus spread at amazing speed. 病毒的传播速度惊人。

  2.  
     (informal) very impressive; excellent 令人大为惊奇的；（尤指）令人惊喜（或惊羡、惊叹）的 synonym fantastic (1)
    • He makes the most amazing cakes. 他做的蛋糕最棒。
    • I saw the most amazing film yesterday! 我昨天看了一场最棒的电影！
    • That looks pretty amazing, doesn't it? 这看起来很神奇，不是吗？
